About Us

StoneDot is a global company specializing in the import and export of premium natural stones including marble, granite, quartzite, travertine, and sandstone. We provide both raw blocks and cut slabs, partnering with some of the world’s leading quarries and stone factories. Our mission is to deliver quality, consistency, and efficiency in every stone shipment — building strong, long-term relationships with clients across the globe.
