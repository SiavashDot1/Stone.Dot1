About

Stone.Dot is an international company active in the import and export of premium natural stones, including marble, granite, quartzite, travertine, and sandstone. We specialize in delivering high-quality blocks and slabs to global markets and proudly partner with some of the world’s most reputable quarries and factories. Our mission is to connect customers with the beauty and durability of nature’s finest materials.
