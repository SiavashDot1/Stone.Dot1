About Us

Stone.Dot is a premium international company specializing in the import and export of natural stones, including marble, granite, quartzite, travertine, and sandstone. We offer both raw blocks and processed slabs, partnering with some of the most prestigious quarries and factories across the globe. Our mission is to deliver timeless quality, precision, and elegance in natural stone, tailored to meet the demands of high-end architecture and interior design markets.
